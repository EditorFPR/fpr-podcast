About the Podcast

On the Record brings you the conversations that matter — with the academics, lawyers, diplomats, civil society leaders and policymakers shaping what comes next for Fiji and the Pacific. Every episode, one guest, one conversation, on the record.

The Publication

The Fiji Political Review is an independent journal of Fijian politics, policy and governance. We publish analysis, investigations, op-eds and briefings. On the Record is our interview podcast.
